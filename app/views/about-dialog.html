About Hyde

HYDE is Jekyll's evil twin. We have lots of data and data can be evil. But every cloud has a silver lining. So HYDE help you make sense of the data to find a great place to live in sunny Singapore, where housing, transport and health are great.

Made with Polymer, AngularJS & Leaflet
Data Sources: data.gov.sg, OneMap, URA, LTA, SLA...
Done by: Kah Khang, Lynnette Ng, Raymond, Ji An
